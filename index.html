About me

My whole life I have had a fascination with technology and computers. From video games to building computers with my dad I have always found fun and challenging ways to work with computers. Funny enough, it wasn't until my final semester of college when I took a random web design course that made me think coding might be the right path for me. It was the first class in years that I felt truly engaged and excited to work on. I was working late on weekend nights to finish projects and was happy to do so!

After a few years of working in digital marketing and a little global pandemic, I finally decided to dive headfirst into computer engineering and enroll with Thinkful. A few weeks in and I have already learned so much and feel the excitement about learning a new skill, challenging myself, and the new possibilities that are available with a new career!

I will be able to provide multiple services including web design, big data analysis, and app development.
